## Grammar Rules Covered

### الحروف الجارة (Prepositions)
- **Definition**: Particles placed with verbs that cause جر (jar case) in the following noun
- **Key Rule**: All nouns following prepositions MUST be in the jar case (most commonly marked with kasra)
- **Alternative Names**: Also called صلة (sila) when used after specific verbs

### 10 Common Prepositions:
1. **في** (fī) - in, regarding
2. **من** (min) - from, than (for comparison)
3. **على** (ʿalā) - on, upon
4. **كـ** (ka) - as, like
5. **بـ** (bi) - with, at, by (for tools/instruments)
6. **إلى** (ilā) - to, up to
7. **لـ** (li) - for, belonging to
8. **حتى** (ḥattā) - until, up to (time/extent)
9. **عن** (ʿan) - about, from, regarding
10. **واو** (wāw) - by (for oaths, e.g., والله)

### 7 Less Frequent Prepositions:
1. **تـ** (tā) - by (for oaths, like والله)
2. **مذ** (mudh) - since, for (time periods)
3. **منذ** (mundhu) - since, for (time periods)
4. **رُبَّ** (rubba) - many, so many (always with singular noun)
5. **خلا** (khalā) - besides, except
6. **حاشا** (ḥāshā) - besides, except
7. **عدا** (ʿadā) - besides, except

### Special Rules:
- **بـ vs. مع**: Use بـ (bi) for tools/instruments, مع (maʿa) for companionship
- **رُبَّ**: Always takes singular noun despite meaning "many"
- **Time expressions**: مذ/منذ used for both "since" (defined time) and "for" (duration)

## I'rab (Sentence Analysis) Examples

1. **ذهب زيد إلى المسجد**
   - إلى: حرف جر
   - المسجد: اسم مجرور بإلى وعلامة جره الكسرة

2. **كتبت بالقلم**
   - بـ: حرف جر
   - القلم: اسم مجرور بالباء وعلامة جره الكسرة

3. **رُبَّ رجلٍ نصرته**
   - رُبَّ: حرف جر
   - رجلٍ: اسم مجرور برُبَّ وعلامة جره الكسرة (singular despite "many" meaning)

## Key Takeaways

1. **Mandatory Jar Case**: All nouns following prepositions must be in jar case - no exceptions
2. **Context Determines Meaning**: Same preposition can have multiple meanings (e.g., من = from/than, بـ = with/at/by)
3. **Verb-Specific Requirements**: Some verbs require specific prepositions (صلة) - must be memorized
4. **Tool vs. Companionship**: Use بـ for tools/instruments, مع for companionship
5. **Memorization Essential**: Preposition usage often doesn't follow set rules - requires memorization
6. **Arabic vs. English Logic**: Follow Arabic grammar rules when translating, not English patterns
