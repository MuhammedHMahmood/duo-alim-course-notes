## Grammar Rules Covered

### Past Tense Verb Conjugation (Fi'l Madhi)
- **Pattern**: fa'ala (he did) conjugated through all 14 forms
- **Key conjugations practiced**:
  - dhahaba (he went)
  - zabaha (he slaughtered)
- **Emphasis**: Students must practice conjugations 100+ times for mastery

### Active vs. Passive Voice
- **Active voice**: Standard verb conjugations
- **Passive voice formation**:
  - First letter receives dhamma (ضمة)
  - Second letter receives kasra (كسرة)
  - Example: akala (he ate) → ukila (it was eaten)

### Person, Number, and Gender Identification
- Students must identify grammatical forms in Arabic terms:
  - jama' mudhakkar ghaib (third person plural masculine)
  - wahid mu'annas ghaib (third person singular feminine)
