## Session Overview

This session completed the discussion of إنّ (inna) and its sisters, then focused extensively on كان (kana) and its sisters - the أفعال ناقصة (af'al naqisa/defective verbs). The class covered conjugation patterns, timing-specific verbs, and practical sentence analysis with these constructions.

## Grammar Rules Covered

### الأفعال الناقصة (Al-Af'al An-Naqisa) - The Defective Verbs

**Definition:** Called ناقصة (naqisa/defective) because they are لازم (lazim) in meaning but require two nouns (اسم and خبر) to complete their meaning, acting like متعدّي (muta'addi) verbs.

**I'rab Rules:** Complete opposite of إنّ وأخواتها:
- اسم كان receives رفع (rafa)  
- خبر كان receives نصب (nasab)

### The 13 Defective Verbs

1. **كان** - was | **يكون** - to become/becoming
2. **صار** - became | **يصير** - to become (shows change of state)
3. **أصبح** - became in the morning
4. **أمسى** - became in the evening  
5. **أضحى** - became at mid-morning
6. **ظلّ** - became/remained during the day
7. **بات** - became/remained during the night
8. **ما دام** - as long as (must be in middle of sentence)
9. **ما زال** - continuously/always
10. **ما بَرِح** - continuously/always
11. **ما فَتِئ** - continuously/always  
12. **ما انفكّ** - continuously/always
13. **ليس** - is not/not (no مضارع form)

### Conjugation Patterns

**Rule for Past Tense Conjugation:**
- Look at مضارع form to determine voweling
- If مضارع has واو (like يكون), use ضمّة from صيغة 6 onward
- If مضارع has ياء (like يصير), use كسرة from صيغة 6 onward

**Examples:**
- كان، كانا، كانوا، كانت، كانتا، **كُنّ** (dhamma because of waw in yakunu)
- صار، صارا، صاروا، صارت، صارتا، **سِرن** (kasra because of ya in yasiru)

## I'rab (Sentence Analysis) Examples

**Example 1:** كان البيت نظيفًا
- **كان** - فعل ناقص
- **البيت** - اسم كان مرفوع  
- **نظيفًا** - خبر كان منصوب

**Example 2:** صار الرجل غنيًا
- **صار** - فعل ناقص
- **الرجل** - اسم صار مرفوع
- **غنيًا** - خبر صار منصوب

**Example 3:** ليس الخادم قويًا
- **ليس** - فعل ناقص للنفي
- **الخادم** - اسم ليس مرفوع
- **قويًا** - خبر ليس منصوب

## Practice Examples

1. **ليس العامل نشيطًا** - The worker is not active
2. **كان منامه منامًا صادقًا** - His dream was a true dream
3. **أمسى الغني فقيرًا** - The rich man became poor (in the evening)
4. **نويت الاعتكاف ما دمت في المسجد** - I intended i'tikaf as long as I was in the mosque
5. **صار الكبش سمينًا** - The ram became fat
6. **لا يزال لسانك رطبًا من ذكر الله** - Your tongue continues to be moist with the remembrance of Allah
7. **يصبح المداد جافًا** - The ink becomes dry

## Key Takeaways

1. **كان** with مضارع gives past continuous or habitual meaning: **كان زيد يكتب** = "Zayd was writing/used to write"

2. **Verb Availability:**
   - **كان** through **بات**: Have ماضي، مضارع، and أمر forms
   - **ما زال** group: Have ماضي and مضارع only (no أمر)
   - **ما دام** and **ليس**: Have ماضي only

3. **Special Rules:**
   - **ما دام** cannot start a sentence
   - **ليس** sometimes takes بـ prefix on خبر for emphasis
   - **الألف المقصورة** converts to ياء during conjugation

4. **Timing-specific verbs** (أصبح، أمسى، أضحى، ظلّ، بات) can be used generally without time restrictions
