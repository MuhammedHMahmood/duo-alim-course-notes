## Fiqh Rulings & Evidence

### When Jamaah Begins While Praying Alone

**For 4-rakah prayers (Zuhr, Asr, Isha):**
- If not yet performed first prostration: Break prayer with one salam and join congregation
- If completed first prostration but not more: Complete second rakah, say salam (counts as 2 nafl), then join congregation for obligatory prayer
- If completed 3 rakahs: Finish all 4 rakahs, then join congregation intending voluntary prayer (except for Asr - no nafl after Asr)

**For Fajr sunnah specifically:**
- Only sunnah prayer allowed while congregation is ongoing
- Only pray if reasonably sure you can catch the congregation
- If imam is in final tashahud, join directly without sunnah

**For 3-rakah prayer (Maghrib):**
- No joining congregation after individual completion (no 3-rakah nafl exists)

### Entering Mosque When Prayer Already Started

- Do not pray any sunnah except 2 rakah before Fajr
- Join congregation immediately for all prayers except Fajr sunnah
- For Fajr sunnah: Only if confident you can catch congregation before it ends

### Merit of Congregation

- Thawab (reward) of congregation attained even by catching final tashahud
- However, person must complete remaining rakahs to fulfill prayer obligation
- Catching ruku means catching that rakah
- Must spend at least a moment in ruku with imam to count the rakah

## Conditions & Categories

### Sajda Sahw Requirements

**When Required:**
- Missing or delaying wajib acts due to forgetfulness or inattentiveness
- NOT for missing fard acts (requires full prayer repetition)
- Must be unintentional mistake

**When NOT Required:**
- If disliked prayer times enter (zawal, sunrise, sunset)
- If prayer time expires
- If done intentionally (prayer becomes invalid)

**Procedure:**
1. Complete prayer normally
2. Say first salam (Assalamu alaykum wa rahmatullahi on right)
3. Perform two sajdahs
4. Sit up and recite complete tashahud
5. Complete with both salams

### Extra Rakah Scenarios

**Standing for extra rakah without final sitting:**
- If no sajda performed yet: Sit immediately and complete
- If sajda of extra rakah performed: Prayer becomes invalid, complete in pairs (add 6th for 4-rakah prayers, 4th for 3-rakah), then repeat entire obligatory prayer

**For Maghrib specifically:**
- If extra rakah performed: Add only one more (total 4) to maintain even numbers
- Must repeat Maghrib prayer afterward

## Scholarly Opinions

### Alternative Views on Sunnah Interruption

- Some scholars allow completing 4 rakahs sunnah regardless of jamaah timing
- Hanafi position: Break after 2 if jamaah begins, complete remaining 2 after obligatory prayer

### Sajda Sahw Timing

- Sunnah: After first salam
- Valid but mildly disliked: Before any salam
- Difference with other madhabs: Hanafi does salam first, others do sajda then salam

### Frequent Doubts in Prayer

**Three categories:**
1. Rare doubts: Repeat entire prayer
2. Occasional doubts: Go with stronger assumption
3. Frequent doubts: Assume minimum certain amount, treat each rakah as potentially the last

## Lessons & Practical Application

### Mosque Etiquette

- If adhan called in mosque, remain until prayer completed (unless valid excuse)
- After completing individual prayer: May leave except for Zuhr and Isha (should join as nafl)
- Congregation takes precedence over individual prayer

### Prayer Management

- When unsure of rakah count: Go with confident assumption or assume minimum if no confidence
- Hair showing during prayer: Fix when alerted (doesn't break prayer), but repeat if discovered after prayer
- Breaking wudu during congregational prayer: Cannot make wudu mid-prayer and return

### Mistake Prevention

- Pay attention during prayer to avoid common errors
- If mistake made unintentionally, follow sajda sahw procedure
- If fundamental pillar (fard) missed, entire prayer must be repeated
- Maintain even numbers when adding extra rakahs due to mistakes

### Following Imam

- Must catch at least a moment in ruku to count rakah
- When making up missed rakahs: Will sit for tashahud multiple times (following both imam's pattern and your own completion pattern)
- If imam makes uncorrected mistake affecting his prayer, entire congregation must repeat
