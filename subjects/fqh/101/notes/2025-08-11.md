## Fiqh Rulings & Evidence

### Definition of Fiqh (Three Scholarly Perspectives):

1. **Imam Abu Hanifa (rahimahullah)**: "Fiqh is a knowledge of a person pertaining to the rulings of what is good and bad for him"

2. **Imam Shafi (rahimahullah)**: "Fiqh is the knowledge of religious practical rulings deduced from the detailed sources"

3. **Majalla (Ottoman Legal Code)**: Defined as matters relating to religious practical affairs

### Wudu Rulings Discussed:
- Hands are washed at the beginning of wudu and are included in the washing of arms up to the elbows
- For tight rings: must be removed or ensure water reaches underneath
- For loose rings: sufficient if certain that water penetrates

## Conditions & Categories

### Primary Categories of Fiqh:
1. **Ibadah (Worship)**
   - **Badaniyya (Physical)**: Acts requiring physical exertion and activity
   - **Maliyya (Financial)**: Acts involving financial obligations (zakah, sadaqah, financial aspects of hajj)

2. **Mu'amalat (Transactions)**: Daily dealings including buying, selling, contracts, oaths, and agreements

### Core Subjects to be Covered:
- **Tahara** (Purification) - prerequisite for many acts of worship
- **Salah** (Prayer)
- **Sawm** (Fasting)  
- **Zakah** (Alms tax)
- **Hajj** (Annual pilgrimage)

## Scholarly Opinions

### Washing After Dog Contact:
- **For skin**: Seven times (established ruling)
- **For clothing**: Three times (instructor needs to verify exact number, noting some hadith mention seven)

### Beard Washing in Wudu:
- **Thick beard**: Wash only the hair directly connected to the face, not the hanging portions
- **Thin beard**: Water must reach the underlying skin of the face and chin

## Lessons & Practical Application

1. **Comprehensive Nature of Fiqh**: Islamic jurisprudence encompasses all aspects of life, not just the five pillars of worship, but extends to all daily transactions and interactions

2. **Practical Wudu Guidelines**:
   - Ensure water reaches all required areas including under loose jewelry
   - Different rules apply for thick vs. thin facial hair
   - Proper washing technique varies based on the area of the body

3. **Study Methodology**: Regular review of fundamental concepts is essential for building comprehensive understanding of Islamic law

4. **Integration of Theory and Practice**: Understanding definitions must be coupled with practical application in daily religious observances
