## Fiqh Rulings & Evidence

### Prayer Timing Differences:
- **Asr timing according to Abu Hanifa**: When shadow = 2 times original + object's shadow
- **Asr timing according to Abu Yusuf & Muhammad**: When shadow = 1 time original + object's shadow
- **Example**: 6-foot person → Abu Yusuf/Muhammad: 12 feet total shadow; Abu Hanifa: 18 feet total shadow

### Forbidden Prayer Times:
Three times when ALL prayers (fard, qada, sajdatul tilawah) are INVALID:
1. At sunrise
2. At midday (zawal)
3. At sunset
- **Exception**: Only Asr of that specific day can be prayed at sunset

### Makroo Times for Voluntary Prayers:
1. The three forbidden times above
2. During Arabic khutbah
3. Before Fajr fard (except 2 rakats sunnah)
4. After Fajr fard until after sunrise
5. After Asr fard until sunset

## Conditions & Categories

### Recommended Prayer Times (Mustahab):

**Fajr**: During isfar (when light begins to appear)
**Zuhr**: 
- Summer: Delay when weather is hot for cooling
- Winter: Pray early after zawal
**Asr**: Delay until before sun changes (can look at sun without harm)
**Maghrib**: Immediately upon sunset
**Isha**: In cloudy weather, pray immediately; otherwise delay somewhat
**Cloudy Weather Rule**: Pray Asr and Isha immediately; delay other prayers

### Adhan Requirements:
- Called in Arabic only
- Caller should be righteous, know proper timing and method
- Should be in state of ritual purity
- Face qibla, fingers in ears
- Turn face right for "Hayya 'ala salah", left for "Hayya 'ala falah"

## Scholarly Opinions

### Prayer Combination Differences:
**Hanafi Position**: 
- Only jamaa suri allowed (except Hajj)
- Pray Zuhr at very end of time, then Asr immediately when time begins
- Actual combination (jamaa haqiqi) only during Hajj at Arafah and Muzdalifah

**Other Madhabs**: Allow actual combination during travel

### Ibadat vs Muamalat:
- **Ibadat (worship matters)**: Follow Imam Abu Hanifa's opinion
- **Muamalat (worldly dealings)**: Follow opinions of Abu Yusuf and Muhammad
- Reasoning: Students' opinions were often Abu Hanifa's earlier positions before he revised them

### Witr Prayer Methods:
- **Hanafi way**: 3 rakats with tashahhud in 2nd, dua qunoot before rukoo in 3rd
- **Other methods**: 2+1 separate, or 3 without middle tashahhud
- All four imams agree on 3 rakats total, differ in methodology

## Lessons & Practical Application

### Community Prayer Situations:
- When praying with family/community following different madhab, respectfully follow your own school
- For witr differences in community: Ask local mufti for guidance, may pray separately at home
- When pressed for time in school/work: Can follow local timing if ensures prayer completion

### Adhan Mistakes to Avoid:
- **Falah** (correct) vs **Falah** (barren land - incorrect)
- **Allahu Akbar** (correct) vs **Allah Akbar** (changes meaning - incorrect) 
- Proper Arabic pronunciation essential for validity

### Travel and Combination:
- Hanafi traveler should use jamaa suri method when needed
- At home: Call Adhan and Iqamah even for individual prayer
- When combining: One Adhan, two Iqamahs for Zuhr-Asr

### Practical Timing Issues:
- True dawn difficult to see in urban areas due to light pollution
- Can rely on calculated prayer times when unable to observe natural signs
- Fajr ends at actual sunrise (sun's disk visible), not just sky illumination
