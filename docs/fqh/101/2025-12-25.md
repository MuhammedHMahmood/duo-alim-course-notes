This session covered the essential rulings of Umrah (lesser pilgrimage), expiation for Hajj/Umrah violations, hunting regulations during Ihram, Udhiyah (sacrifice) requirements, and general slaughtering principles. ## Fiqh Rulings & Evidence

**Umrah Timing:**
- Valid throughout the year except five days (9th-13th Dhul Hijjah)
- Prohibitively disliked (Makroo Tahrimi) during Hajj days
- Best day is Arafah falling on Friday (worth more than 70 Hajj)

**Umrah Components:**
- Tawaf: Seven rounds around Kaaba
- Two Rakat after Tawaf
- Sa'i: Seven rounds between Safa and Marwah
- Shaving/trimming hair to exit Ihram

**Zakat Nisab:**
- Measured by gold (~$9,400) or silver (~$700) standards
- Standards apply universally regardless of local currency value

**Slaughtering Requirements:**
- Must cut minimum 3 out of 4 vessels: trachea, esophagus, two carotid arteries
- Basmala required unless forgotten unintentionally
- Slaughterer must be Muslim, Christian, or Jew (not Majin, apostate, or idol worshiper)

## Conditions & Categories

**Ihram Violations Requiring Sheep Sacrifice:**
- Applying scent to entire limb
- Wearing stitched garment for full day/night (men)
- Covering quarter of head for full day/night (men)
- Shaving quarter of head/beard
- Clipping all nails in one sitting
- Missing entire day of stoning

**Lesser Violations (Half Sa' of wheat):**
- Applying scent to less than entire limb
- Wearing stitched garment for less than full day
- Performing Tawaf in state of impurity

**Animal Age Requirements for Sacrifice:**
- Camel: 5 years (Thani)
- Cow: 2 years
- Goat: 1 year
- Sheep: 6 months (if appears mature)

**Prohibited Animals for Sacrifice:**
- One-eyed or blind animals
- Emaciated animals
- Missing ears or most of tail
- Cannot walk to slaughter place
- Missing most teeth

## Scholarly Opinions

**Hanafi Position on Nisab:**
- Difference of opinion exists regarding silver vs. gold nisab standards
- Generally follows whichever is more beneficial to the poor

**Imam Abu Yusuf vs. Imam Muhammad:**
- Abu Yusuf: Animal needs most of day's life remaining after injury
- Muhammad: Any sign of life sufficient for slaughter validity

**Horse Meat Ruling:**
- Imam Abu Hanifa: Prohibitively disliked
- Two Companions: Mildly disliked

## Lessons & Practical Application

**Umrah Renewal Process:**
- Travel to Masjid Aisha (main Miqat for residents/visitors)
- Perform two Rakat, make intention
- Return in Ihram state for new Umrah

**Practical Slaughtering Guidelines:**
- Sharpen blade beforehand, away from animal's sight
- Look for two blood streams and visible esophagus opening
- Cover animal's eyes with ears for comfort
- Avoid vertical cutting for cattle/sheep

**Exam Preparation Advice:**
- Review daily, divide chapters manageable portions
- Practice with someone testing you
- Recite "Alam Nashrah" before exam
- When stuck, recite Durud to jog memory

**Precautionary Measures:**
- Set aside money for unintentional Ihram violations
- Give to workers in Haram as expiation
- Better to seek forgiveness than risk incomplete observance
